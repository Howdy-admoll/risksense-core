# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone who participates in the RiskSense Core community will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Values

We value:
- **Inclusivity** — We welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and experiences
- **Respect** — We treat all members with courtesy and professionalism
- **Collaboration** — We believe great solutions come from diverse perspectives
- **Transparency** — We communicate openly and honestly
- **Accountability** — We take responsibility for our words and actions

## Expected Behavior

### Be Respectful
- Treat all people with respect and dignity
- Listen actively to others' viewpoints
- Disagree constructively and professionally
- Use inclusive language

### Be Collaborative
- Work together toward common goals
- Share knowledge and expertise
- Help others learn and grow
- Credit contributions appropriately

### Be Professional
- Maintain professional communication in all interactions
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Respect others' privacy and confidentiality
- Focus on the work, not personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is not tolerated:

- **Harassment** — Unwelcome comments, messages, or actions based on protected characteristics
- **Discrimination** — Unfair treatment based on identity, background, or beliefs
- **Intimidation** — Threatening language or behavior
- **Abuse** — Offensive, derogatory, or hostile communication
- **Exclusion** — Deliberately excluding or isolating individuals
- **Violation of Privacy** — Sharing private information without consent
- **Retaliation** — Punishing someone for reporting violations

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project repositories and issue trackers
- Project communications (email, chat, forums)
- Project events and meetings
- Public representations of the project

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation:

1. **Document** — Note the incident (date, time, people involved, description)
2. **Report** — Contact: admoll.adefemi@gmail.com
3. **Be Specific** — Include details about what happened and impact
4. **Privacy** — Your report will be treated confidentially

**Please do not discuss the incident publicly** while it's being investigated.

## Investigation & Response

When a violation is reported:

1. **Confidentiality** — The incident is reviewed privately
2. **Fairness** — All parties have opportunity to provide context
3. **Timeliness** — We investigate promptly
4. **Action** — Appropriate steps are taken (warning, suspension, removal)

Possible responses include:
- Private conversation or warning
- Temporary suspension from project
- Permanent removal from project
- Escalation to appropriate authorities (if legal violations)

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of behavior
- Addressing inappropriate behavior promptly
- Investigating reports fairly and confidentially
- Keeping community safe and inclusive
